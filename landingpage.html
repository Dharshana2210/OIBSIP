Margherita

Classic delight with 100% real mozzarella cheese.Pizza Margherita is a typical Neapolitan pizza, roundish in shape with a raised edge (the cornicione) and garnished with hand-crushed peeled tomatoes, mozzarella ( buffalo mozzarella or fior di latte ), fresh basil leaves, and extra virgin olive oil.

Pepperoni

Spicy pepperoni with a blend of cheese and tomato sauce.Sliced pepperoni is one of the most popular pizza toppings in American pizzerias. Traditionally made pepperonis curl into "cups" in the pizza oven's intense heat; commercialization of the production of pepperoni created slices that would lie flat on the pie. The curled "cup and char" style of pepperoni remained popular in pockets of the Midwest .
